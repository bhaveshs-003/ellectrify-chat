Meet the Ellectrify Concierge

This page is a working proof of concept for an on-site chat assistant that guides shoppers to the right grounding product and answers common support questions. The conversation runs on hardcoded logic in the browser — there's no live AI or backend.

  1. Open the chat using the bubble in the bottom-right corner (it also opens automatically).
  2. Pick your main goal — Better sleep, Less stress, Aches & recovery, or Travel — and the concierge recommends a product you can add to cart.
  3. Tap a follow-up like “Which size?”, “Is it safe?”, or “Track my order”, or type your own message.
  4. Flip Support mode in the header to switch the replies to order status, returns, and troubleshooting.
  5. Use the language menu (English / Español / Deutsch) to see the greeting adapt.

Wellness guidance only — not medical advice. Pricing, products, and responses shown here are illustrative sample data.
